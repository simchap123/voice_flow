Open VoiceFlow in 3 steps

Get up and running in under a minute.

1

Open your Downloads

Look for VoiceFlow Setup.exe in your Downloads folder or at the bottom of your browser.

2

Double-click VoiceFlow Setup.exe

If Windows SmartScreen appears, click "More info" then "Run anyway" — the app is safe and open source.

3

Wait for it to launch — then start dictating

VoiceFlow will appear in your system tray. Hold Alt from anywhere to start your first dictation — release to stop. You can also configure a toggle hotkey in Settings.

Set up your API key

VoiceFlow uses your own API key for transcription and text cleanup. Your key is encrypted on your device and never sent to us.

Groq (recommended — fast & cheap):

  1. Go to console.groq.com/keys
  2. Create an API key and copy it
  3. In VoiceFlow Settings, select Groq as your provider and paste the key

OpenAI (alternative):

  1. Go to platform.openai.com/api-keys
  2. Click "Create new secret key" and copy it
  3. In VoiceFlow Settings, select OpenAI as your provider and paste the key

That's it! Groq is ~8x cheaper than OpenAI. Typical usage costs less than a penny per day.
