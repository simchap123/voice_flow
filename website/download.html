Open VoxGen in 3 steps

Get up and running in under a minute.

1

Open your Downloads

Look for VoxGen Setup.exe in your Downloads folder or at the bottom of your browser.

2

Run the installer

Double-click VoxGen-Setup.exe. If Windows SmartScreen appears, click "More info" then "Run anyway" — the app is safe. Follow the installer steps and wait for it to finish.

3

Launch VoxGen — start dictating

VoxGen launches automatically after install. Look for the purple mic icon in your system tray (bottom-right of your screen). Hold Alt from any app to dictate — release to stop.

Not seeing the tray icon? Try restarting your computer, or find VoxGen in your Start menu.

Set up your API key

VoxGen uses your own API key for transcription and text cleanup. Your key is encrypted on your device and never sent to us.

Groq (recommended — fast & cheap):

  1. Go to console.groq.com/keys
  2. Create an API key and copy it
  3. In VoxGen Settings, select Groq as your provider and paste the key

OpenAI (alternative):

  1. Go to platform.openai.com/api-keys
  2. Click "Create new secret key" and copy it
  3. In VoxGen Settings, select OpenAI as your provider and paste the key

That's it! Groq is ~8x cheaper than OpenAI. Typical usage costs less than a penny per day.
