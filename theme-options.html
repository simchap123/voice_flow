VoxGen — Redesign Direction

Tagline + app theme + website style — pick a direction for each.

Step 1
New tagline (replace "Type with your voice")
OPTION 1
Your voice, perfected.
Three words. Premium feel. Emphasizes AI cleanup — your raw speech becomes polished text. Completely different angle from every competitor.
OPTION 2
Speak freely. Write perfectly.
Parallel structure. "Freely" = natural speech, "perfectly" = AI polish. Shows the transformation gap that VoxGen bridges. Aspirational.
OPTION 3
Say it rough. Get it polished.
Honest, casual, direct. Speaks to the real experience — you ramble, VoxGen cleans it up. Nobody else is owning this positioning.
Competitors to avoid: "Don't type, just speak" (Wispr Flow) • "Speak, don't type" (Typeless) • "Effortless voice dictation" (Wispr) • "Type with your voice" (current VoxGen — too close)
Step 2
App UI + matching website — three calm directions
A Frost Clean B&W, Notion-like restraint
